Find the first derivative of each of the following functions.

(a) $y=4+2 x$;

(b) $y=4+2 x+x^{2}$;

(c) $y=4-\sqrt{x}$;

(d) $y=8+x^{\frac{5}{2}}-x^{-\left(\frac{3}{2}\right)}$;

(e) $y=x^{4}+2 e^{x}$;

(f) $y=10 \ln (x)$;

(g) $C=10+8 Q+\frac{2}{Q}$;

(h) $R=10-5 Q+Q^{\frac{3}{2}}$;

(i) $\Pi=-5+10 Q+0.5 Q^{3}$; and

(j) $y=(1.08)^{x}+10$.
